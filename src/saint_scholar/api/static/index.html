About Saint & Scholar

What is this?

Saint & Scholar is a conversational tool that lets you explore science through the voices of history’s great thinkers. Ask a question about neuroscience, psychology, genetics, or any topic in the knowledge base—and a historical figure responds using real, peer-reviewed research.

How it works

  1. Pick a voice. Choose a historical or philosophical figure from the bar at the top.
  2. Ask a question. Type anything you’re curious about—the more specific, the better.
  3. Get a grounded answer. The response draws from PubMed abstracts and cites its sources, so you can verify everything.

The knowledge base

Answers are grounded in abstracts pulled from PubMed, covering neuroscience, psychology, genetics, longevity, ecology, nutrition, immunology, consciousness, and more. The corpus is regularly updated.

The voices

Each figure’s speaking style is drawn from their public-domain philosophical or spiritual texts. The voice shapes how the answer is delivered, not what it says—the science stays the same regardless of who’s speaking.

A note on accuracy

Every answer is grounded in real papers, but this is an AI tool—not a substitute for expert advice. Citations and links to original sources are provided so you can always check for yourself.
